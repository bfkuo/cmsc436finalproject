Foreign Language Text Recognizer is an app intended for helping travelers navigate in a foreign country. The app will recognize foreign text that appears in a picture that users provide, either by taking a picture from within the app or by uploading a picture to the app from the camera roll, then translate the text into the userâ€™s preferred language and replace the original text with the translated text.
